# Privacy Policy for SecuriScan

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## Overview

SecuriScan is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how SecuriScan handles information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

- No personal information collected
- No browsing history tracked
- No data sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking
- No cookies set by the extension
- No user accounts required

## How the Extension Works

SecuriScan performs all security analysis locally in your browser:

1. When you click "Scan," the extension analyzes the current webpage
2. All analysis happens on your device
3. Results are displayed only to you
4. Exported reports are saved locally to your computer
5. Cached results are stored only in your browser's local storage

## Permissions Explained

- **activeTab**: Access the current tab to perform security analysis
- **scripting**: Inject analysis script into the page
- **storage**: Cache scan results locally in your browser
- **host_permissions**: Allow scanning on any website you choose

## Third-Party Services

SecuriScan does NOT use any third-party services. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising networks, no external APIs.

## Open Source

SecuriScan analyzes websites locally in your browser. We collect zero data, track nothing, and send no information anywhere. Your privacy is completely protected.
